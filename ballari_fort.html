Description:

Ballari Fort, situated on top of a monolithic hill in Ballari, Karnataka, is a historic fort with remarkable architecture and a rich past. Built during the Vijayanagara Empire and later modified by Hyder Ali in the 18th century, the fort is divided into two partsâ€”the Upper Fort and the Lower Fort. The Upper Fort offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and features impressive defensive structures, while the Lower Fort houses several administrative buildings and temples.
