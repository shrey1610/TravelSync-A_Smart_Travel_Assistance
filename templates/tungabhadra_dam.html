Welcome to Tungabhadra Dam

Enjoy scenic views at Tungabhadra Dam.

Description:

Tungabhadra Dam (TB Dam) is a multipurpose dam built across the Tungabhadra River in Hosapete, Ballari district. The dam is one of the significant attractions for visitors to Hampi. The Tunga Bhadra reservoir has a storage capacity of 101 TMC, with its catchment area covering 28,000 square kilometers. The dam stands at a height of 49.5 meters and has about 33 crest gates.
