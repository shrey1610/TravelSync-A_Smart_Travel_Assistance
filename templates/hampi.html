Welcome to Hampi

Discover the ancient ruins of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Description:

Hampi or Vijayanagara, known as Kishkindha in the Ramayana age, is a town in the Vijayanagara district in the Indian state of Karnataka. Located along the Tungabhadra River in the east and center part of the state, Hampi is near the city of Hosapete.

District:

Vijayanagara

Elevation:

467 m (1,532 ft)

Hours:

7 AM - 6 PM

Best Visiting Timings

Early morning (6 AM - 9 AM) or late afternoon (4 PM - 6 PM) to avoid the heat and enjoy beautiful sunrise or sunset views.
