Explore the beautiful Kumaraswamy Temple!

Description:

Kumaraswamy Temple, located in Krauncha Giri near Sandur in Karnataka, is a historic temple complex dating back to the 8th and 10th centuries. The temple is known for its ancient architecture and is situated about 10 km from Sandur in Bellary District. It features two Hindu temples within the same compound, both of which are protected monuments of historical significance.
